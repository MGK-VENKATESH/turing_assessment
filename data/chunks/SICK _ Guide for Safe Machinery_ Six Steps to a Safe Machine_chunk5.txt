This guide is aimed at manufacturers, users, designers, system engineers, and all individuals who are responsible for machine safety. Use of the terms “safety” and “safe” in this document According to ISO Guide 51, "The term “safe” is often understood by the general public as the state of being protected from all hazards. However, this is a misunderstanding: “safe” is rather the state of being protected from recognized hazards that are likely to cause harm”.
