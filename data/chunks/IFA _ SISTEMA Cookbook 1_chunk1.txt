Introduction 1 Introduction Control systems which perform safety functions are employed in order for machines to be designed safely and thereby to satisfy the requirements of the Machinery Directive, 2006/42/EC. For this purpose, the safety functions required for risk reduction are defined as part of the risk assessment conducted during design of the machine and then implemented by means of a suitable control system.
